Prime Minister Mark Carney reviews first year, sets 2026 goals

Category: Canada

In year-end interviews, Prime Minister Mark Carney looked back on his decision to enter politics, describing it as coming at an “important time,” and reflected on the early direction of his government [2]. Carney told Global News he believes Canada is in a better place than when he first became prime minister, while stressing that the country still needs to keep moving forward on economic growth [3]. Together, the interviews frame his message heading into 2026 as one of steady progress paired with continued work on priorities Canadians care about, especially the economy [2][3].
