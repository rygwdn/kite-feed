Man and child found dead after tractor sinks

Category: Canada

Quebec provincial police say a tractor went through the ice on Lac Ernest in Saint-Zénon in the Lanaudière region on Christmas Eve, with a man and a child onboard [3][4]. After an initial report that the pair were missing, police later confirmed both bodies were recovered Thursday morning, and they were found inside the tractor [2][4][5]. The Sûreté du Québec is investigating the circumstances of the incident [4][6].
