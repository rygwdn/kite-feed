Environment Canada issues Christmas storm warnings across Canada

Category: Canada

Environment Canada advisories and warnings covered wide swaths of the country over Christmas Day, with winter storm and blizzard conditions forecast for parts of Newfoundland and Labrador and northeastern Quebec, and extreme cold flagged across much of Canada [1][2][3]. In Newfoundland and Labrador, strong winds and heavy snowfall along coastal areas created difficult travel and visibility conditions, and power outages left thousands without electricity [1][3]. Elsewhere, western provinces faced bitter wind chills, while parts of Ontario dealt with freezing rain risk—different hazards, same message: plan carefully and give yourself extra time if you must be out [1][2][3].
