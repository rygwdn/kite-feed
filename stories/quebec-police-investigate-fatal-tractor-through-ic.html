Quebec police investigate fatal tractor-through-ice lake accident

Category: Canada

Quebec provincial police say an adult man and a child were found dead inside a tractor in Lac Ernest after it went through the ice in the Lanaudière region [1][3][5][2]. The incident occurred in Saint-Zénon, northeast of Montreal, and prompted an emergency response and search after a family member witnessed the accident and called police around 6 p.m. [4][3]. Authorities are investigating the circumstances of the crash, a reminder that early-winter ice can be unpredictable and that quick reporting by witnesses is crucial in emergencies [1][4].
