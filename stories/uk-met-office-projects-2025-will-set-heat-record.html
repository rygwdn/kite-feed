UK Met Office projects 2025 will set heat record

Category: Science

UK Met Office forecasters say 2025 is likely to become the UK’s hottest year since records began, with the year’s average temperature tracking ahead of the current record set in 2022 [1][2]. Both reports link the outlook to a year that featured heatwaves, drought and a notably mild autumn, while noting that late-year weather can still influence the final ranking [2]. A UK climate adviser told the BBC that the trend signals what the country should expect more often as the climate warms, underscoring the value of forecasting and preparedness [1].
