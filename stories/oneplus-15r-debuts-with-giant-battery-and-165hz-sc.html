OnePlus 15R debuts with giant battery and 165Hz screen

Category: Technology

OnePlus has launched the 15R, a mid-range Android phone that brings a 7,400mAh battery, a 6.83-inch 165Hz AMOLED display, and Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chip to a $699.99 starting price in the US, with open sales set for January 8, 2026 and similar January availability in Canada.[androidauthority.com#1][pcmag.com#1][mobilesyrup.com#1] Reviewers widely praise its exceptional stamina and strong performance, with some tests reporting over 23 hours of continuous battery benchmarking or more than 13 hours of screen-on time in mixed real-world use.[androidheadlines.com#1][pcmag.com#1] To hit this price and make room for the huge battery, OnePlus omits a telephoto camera, keeps a basic 8MP ultrawide, and forgoes features like wireless charging and an LTPO display, while also slowing US wired charging to 55W compared to earlier R-series models.[androidauthority.com#1][androidauthority.com#2][androidheadlines.com#2] The result is a "battery-first" device that undercuts many Samsung, Google, and Apple flagships on price but draws mixed reviews over its camera system, rising cost relative to past R phones, and software direction.[techspot.com#1][zdnet.com#1][androidauthority.com#2]

The OnePlus 15R delivers the battery life you’ve always wanted, flagship-level performance that stays cool under pressure, and a premium design—all without breaking the bank.

— Alexander Maxham (Android Headlines)

Did you know: The OnePlus 15R’s battery is larger than that of the already battery-focused OnePlus 15, despite the latter previously being described as having "class leading" endurance in tests.[pcmag.com#1][androidheadlines.com#2]

Key Points

  • Pricing and storage: In the US, the OnePlus 15R starts at $699.99 for 12GB of RAM and 256GB of storage, with a 12GB/512GB option at $799, while in Canada the base 12GB/256GB model is listed at CA$999.99.[androidauthority.com#1][androidheadlines.com#2][mobilesyrup.com#1]
  • Durability upgrades: The phone carries extensive dust and water protection, matching the OnePlus 15 with IP66, IP68, IP69, and IP69K certifications, meaning it is rated to handle both immersion and high-pressure heated water jets.[engadget.com#1][androidheadlines.com#2]
  • Biometrics and controls: OnePlus replaces its traditional Alert Slider with a new Plus Key for customizable actions and adds an ultrasonic in-display fingerprint sensor that aims to improve reliability over optical solutions.[engadget.com#1][androidauthority.com#1]
  • Display limitations: Despite its high 165Hz refresh rate, reviewers note that only select games can currently take advantage of this maximum speed and the panel lacks LTPO tech, so it cannot dynamically scale its refresh rate as efficiently as some rival flagships.[androidauthority.com#1][androidheadlines.com#1]
  • Camera configuration: The 15R’s rear camera array consists of a 50MP primary sensor and an 8MP ultrawide, with no dedicated telephoto module, while the front camera is upgraded to 32MP with autofocus compared to the previous generation.[androidheadlines.com#2][androidauthority.com#1]

Technical Details

  • Snapdragon 8 Gen 5: The OnePlus 15R debuts Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 in a shipping phone, offering high-end CPU and GPU performance that is slightly below Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 and Dimensity 9500 in benchmarks but still comfortably in flagship territory for gaming and multitasking.[androidheadlines.com#1][androidheadlines.com#2]
  • 7,400mAh dual-cell battery: To achieve its long runtimes, the 15R uses a 7,400mAh battery—around 23% larger than the 13R’s pack—which in testing supports more than 13 hours of real-world screen-on usage or over 23 hours in PCMag’s standardized drain test.[androidheadlines.com#1][androidheadlines.com#2][pcmag.com#1]
  • Ultrasonic fingerprint sensor: Instead of an optical in-display fingerprint reader, the phone adopts an ultrasonic sensor that uses sound waves to map the user’s fingerprint, which can improve accuracy and work better with damp fingers compared to many optical solutions.[androidauthority.com#1][common]

Industry Impact

  • Mid-range flagship segment: By pairing a very large battery and flagship-level processor at a sub-premium price, the OnePlus 15R pressures other Android makers to reconsider how much battery capacity and endurance they offer in their own mid-priced "flagship killer" devices, especially in North America where large-battery options are more limited.[androidauthority.com#1][zdnet.com#1]
  • Component and feature trade-offs: The device highlights a broader industry trend of balancing expensive features—such as advanced water resistance and high-refresh displays—against camera hardware and wireless charging, signalling that some brands are willing to prioritize durability and performance over imaging in certain product tiers.[engadget.com#1][androidauthority.com#1][androidheadlines.com#1]

Historical Background

OnePlus’s R-series phones have recently built a reputation for delivering near-flagship performance at noticeably lower prices, with the 2024 OnePlus 12R praised as one of the best $500 Android phones and the 13R regarded as a strong $600 follow-up.[androidauthority.com#2] This year’s 15R continues the idea of a cheaper companion to the main flagship line, but it arrives with a $100 price increase over its predecessor and changes that include dropping the telephoto camera, which shifts expectations for what an "R" model offers relative to earlier generations.[androidauthority.com#1][androidauthority.com#2][androidheadlines.com#2]

Q&A

Q: How does the Plus Key on the OnePlus 15R work in everyday use?

A: The Plus Key replaces OnePlus’s classic Alert Slider and functions similarly to an action button, letting users launch specific apps or actions—like opening the camera or toggling flashlight—through customizable shortcuts rather than just switching between sound profiles.[engadget.com#1]

Q: What role do the multiple IP ratings on the 15R actually play?

A: The phone’s combination of IP66, IP68, IP69, and IP69K ratings means it offers dust-tight protection, resistance to immersion in water, and protection from powerful high-temperature water jets, which is more robust than the single IP68 rating seen on many competing phones.[engadget.com#1][androidheadlines.com#2]

Q: How does the 15R’s camera performance compare to the OnePlus 13R in practice?

A: While both feature a 50MP main sensor, reviewers note that losing the dedicated telephoto lens on the 15R reduces its versatility for zoom shots compared with the 13R, making it more reliant on digital crop from the main camera for 2x or higher magnification.[androidauthority.com#1][androidheadlines.com#2]
