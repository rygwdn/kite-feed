IIO probes Surrey police cruiser crash killing pedestrian

Category: Canada

A woman died after being struck by a Surrey Police Service vehicle in Whalley, Surrey, on Christmas morning, and B.C.’s Independent Investigations Office (IIO) has opened an investigation [1][2][3][4]. Surrey police said the incident occurred around 6 a.m. near 132 Street and 108 Avenue, and the woman later died in hospital [1]. The IIO, as B.C.’s civilian police watchdog, confirmed investigators were sent to the scene, while police said more information would be provided soon [1].

  • Leaving station: One report described the crash as involving a Surrey police vehicle leaving a gas station when the pedestrian was hit [3].
